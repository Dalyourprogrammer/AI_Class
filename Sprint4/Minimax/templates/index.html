Rules
  • Each turn has 3 steps: move (0–2 squares), act, then move remaining squares.
  • Split movement — e.g. move 1 step, attack, then move 1 more step.
  • Fire Bolt — 1 damage if you share a row/column with no column blocking.
  • Dagger — 2 damage if you're adjacent (dist 1) at attack time.
  • Heal Potion — restores 2 HP. One use per battle.
  • Dash — move up to 2 more squares after acting (click purple cell).
  • Both sides start with 3 HP. First to 0 HP loses. You go first.
