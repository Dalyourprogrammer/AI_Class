How to Play

The board is a 3×3 grid of smaller 3×3 Tic-Tac-Toe boards. You are X and go first. On your first move you may click any cell.

The position you choose within a small board determines which small board your opponent must play on next. For example, clicking the top-left cell of any board sends the opponent to the top-left board.

Win a small board to claim that position on the large board. Win three in a row on the large board to win the game! If you are sent to a completed board, you may choose any active board freely.

The AI (O) uses Monte Carlo Tree Search with UCB1 to find its moves.
